Scale of the Universe Quiz

A quiz website where users are tested on their knowledge on the scale of the universe. The questions help the user realize how big our universe really is. Thinking about the scale of universe usually helps me put the world into perspective and calms me down. This app is to help others do the same.

HTML, CSS, Javascript

Healthify

Healthify is a simple app where you can find common substitutes for ingredients in recipes. This is an app to help those with dietary restrictions or those worried that their daily diet is unbalanced. Being a vegetarian, substitutes are very common to my life. But besides that learning how versatile yogurt is as a substitute also led me to make this app (it can substitute for cream in Indian gravy, eggs in baking, etc). Cooking is a science, and this app provides the equations.

HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery
